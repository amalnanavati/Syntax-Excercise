Syntax Exercixe:

This exercise has three types of questions.  The first type gives you a piece of code, and asks you to use dropdowns to select the correct syntactic elements.  The second type gives you a piece of code, and asks you to click on the syntactic elements that are incorrect.  And the third gives you two pieces of code that are all the same except for white space, and asks you to determine the functionality of the code.

When you are done with a question, click the "Submit" button in the bottom-right corner.  The exercise will then tell you how you did on that question.  When you are done reviewing the question, click "Next" (this button will only be visible after you submit) to mvoe on to the next question.

Good luck!
